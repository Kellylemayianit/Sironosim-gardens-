Who We Are

Born in Kimana,
rooted in community

Sironosim Gardens Hotel started as a family dream — a place where travellers heading to Amboseli could rest, eat well, and feel genuinely cared for. Since opening our gates, we have grown from a modest guesthouse into a full-service hotel with comfortable accommodation and a catering team we are deeply proud of.

Our kitchen is led by local cooks who know how Kenyan food should taste — bold, generous, and made with ingredients sourced from the Kimana area whenever possible. Whether you are staying overnight or hiring us for an event, every meal is prepared fresh.

We also offer off-site catering for weddings, funerals, corporate gatherings, and community events throughout Kajiado County. Send us your requirements on WhatsApp and we will build a custom menu around your budget and occasion.
